ONE-SENTENCE DESCRIPTION
------------------------
Grimoire of Blanks is a 1–4 player roguelite spellcrafting deckbuilder where cards are unfinished sentences and the words placed in their blanks decide what happens next.

SHORT DESCRIPTION
-----------------
Every spell begins with blanks. Fill them with words from your hand to decide what the card does, who it works on, and how far you want to push it. Play alone or with up to four players, build a personal spellbook, and fight through ritual seals, events, shops, elites, and bosses.

KEY FEATURES
------------
- Write spells during combat by placing words into unfinished card sentences.
- Combine spell frames, words, trinkets, statuses, and class mechanics.
- Play solo or in online co-op with up to four players.
- Choose from Scribe, Binder, Heretic, and Starmage.
- Shape each run through battles, events, shops, upgrades, elites, and bosses.
