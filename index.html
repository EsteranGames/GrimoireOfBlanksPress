The hook

You do not collect spells. You write them.

Every spell begins with blanks. Fill them with words from your hand to decide what the card does, who it works on, and how far you want to push it. A simple fire spell can become a room-wide burn, a trap that waits for the right enemy intent, or a forbidden burst of power that asks for something back.

Play alone or bring up to four players into the same run. Each mage builds a personal spellbook while the group fights through ritual seals, events, shops, upgrades, elites, and bosses.

Key features

Magic with moving parts

01

Write spells in combat

Drag words into unfinished card sentences. Effects, targets, conditions, and modifiers can turn the same frame into a completely different spell.

02

Build dangerous engines

Combine cards, words, trinkets, class mechanics, and enemy intents into strange interactions—and decide when the payoff is worth the consequence.

03

Fight as a party

Online co-op is built into the run. Up to four players bring separate classes and spellbooks into shared turn-based battles.

04

Choose a school

Scribe, Binder, Heretic, and Starmage each reshape the spell system through repetition, control, sacrifice, or astral power.
